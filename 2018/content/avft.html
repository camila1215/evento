Publication for the 1st Symposium on Business, Health, Human and Social Sciences

You can publicate your articles on the following journals. For the publication process, please read instructions carefully in order to avoid inconveniences.

Publication on AVFT-Archivos Venezolanos de Farmacología y Terapéutica

Conditions for publication on AVFT-Archivos Venezolanos de Farmacología y Terapéutica:

Your paper has to be accepted in the 1st Symposium on Business, Health, Human and Social Sciences only in the topic of Health and sport sciences.
It is recommended to mention 4 works of AVFT-Archivos Venezolanos de Farmacología y Terapéutica, of RLH - Latinoamericana de Hipertensión, Síndrome Cardiometabólico y enfermedades crónico regenerativas or in Diabetes Internacional y endocrinología , of authors different from you.


You can download full journal instructions for publication here.

Once your article is approved, you must send it to the e-mail semanainternacionalsies@gmail.com adjusting to the event schedule.

Publication on Revista Clío América

Conditions for publication on Revista Clío América:

Your paper has to be accepted in the 1st Symposium on Business, Health, Human and Social Sciences in the topics of

  • Social studies, politics, and Law
  • Business, management, and finance
  • International relations and economics
  • Gastronomy and Tourism

Once your article is approved, you must send it to the e-mail semanainternacionalsies@gmail.com adjusting to the event schedule.


Please read all documents annexed below before submitting your article to Revista Clío América.

Guide for autors: Download here.
Normas APA 6th Edition March 2017: Download here.
Format for transferring rights to Revista Clío América: Download here.
Letter to declare intention for publication on Revista Clío América: Download here.

Publication on Revista Magis

Conditions for publication on Revista Magis:

Your paper has to be accepted in the 1st Symposium on Business, Health, Human and Social Sciences in the topics of

  • Pedagogical, didactic practices and educational management
  • Arts and culture studies
  • Humanities sciences, and communication
  • Social sciences, and communication
  • Education, language, literature, and linguistics


Please read all documents annexed below before submitting your article to Revista Magis.

Guide for autors: http://revistas.javeriana.edu.co/index.php/MAGIS/about/submissions

Once your article is approved, you must elaborate the extensive and submit it through the link http://revistas.javeriana.edu.co/index.php/MAGIS/about/submissions and send copy to the e-mail semanainternacionalsies@gmail.com
