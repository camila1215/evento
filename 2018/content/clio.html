Publication on Revista Clío América

Conditions for publication on Revista Clío América:

Your paper has to be accepted in the 1st Symposium on Business, Health, Human and Social Sciences in the topics of

  • Social studies, politics, and Law
  • Business, management, and finance
  • International relations and economics
  • Gastronomy and Tourism

Once your article is approved, you must send it to the e-mail semanainternacionalsies@gmail.com adjusting to the event schedule.
