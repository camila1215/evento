Participate

You can participate in the VI International Week of Science, Technology and Innovation in the form of oral presentation or poster presentation, for which a summary of the research must be presented.

The results to be socialized in oral and poster modality correspond to completed investigations and research advances.

The poster modality must contain the following elements: title, introduction, objectives, theoretical reference, methodology, results, conclusions and bibliography. The logo of the institution presenting the research results and the logo of the Universidad Francisco de Paula Santander Cúcuta and Ocaña should also be included at the top. The size will be 100 cm width x 120 cm height.
For more clear instructions about the poster: Click here.

To download the poster format: Click here.

To download the presentation format: Click here.

The papers approved to participate in the event will have an investment according to the cost of submission for publication in the selected journal to present the research results.

Allied magazines and other product options associated with the event will be available from August 15, 2019.
