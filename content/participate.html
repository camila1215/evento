Participate

You can participate in the 7th IWSTI, or 1st WEA, or 1st ESA, with an oral presentation or poster presentation, for which an abstract of the research must be presented. The results to be socialized in oral and poster modality correspond to completed investigations and research advances. The oral o poster presentations must have the following information: title, introduction, objectives, methodology, results, conclusions, and references.

  • Download the poster presentation template: Here.
  • Download the oral presentation template: Here.
  • For more instructions about the poster: Here.

The research works approved to participate in the workshop will have an investment according to the cost of submission for possible publication in the Conference Series, IOP Publishing.
For the seventh version of the event in 2020, the organizing committee will hold the 7th IWSTI through hybrid participation. It is important to inform you that the corresponding author of each research work approved by the conference, must sending a pre-recorded video of the presentation on the established date according to the following indications: oral presentation (15 minutes), poster presentation (5 minutes). The abstracts presented in the conference will be published in the digital book correspond to the 7th IWSTI with electronic ISSN.
