1st Workshop on Engineering and Applications
(1st WEA)

Is an event aimed at the academic- scientific community and the productive sectors of the region, whose purpose is the divulgation of the advances in research and extension developed in the field of science and engineering of materials by the research laboratories, groups, and centers, promoting so the knowledge transfer and exchange of experiences of research, extension, technological development and innovation with researchers from the National and International

  • Environment and energy
  • Structural and mechanical

1st Workshop on Environmental Science and Applications (1st ESA)

Is an event aimed at the academic-scientific community and the productive sectors of the region, whose purpose is the divulgation of the advances in research and extension developed in the field of agricultural, earth and environmental sciences by the research laboratories, groups, and centers, promoting so the knowledge transfer and exchange of experiences of research, extension, technological development and innovation with researchers from the National and International..

  • Food
  • Pollution
  • Biodiversity
  • Water resources
  • Natural resources
  • Environmental health
  • Mineralogy and metrology
  • Hydrogeology and hydrology
  • Policy and law environmental
  • Environmental risk assessment
  • Climatology and paleoclimatology
  • Atmospheric science and meteorology
